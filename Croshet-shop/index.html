Our Story

Cozy Crafts began as a passion project in a small apartment filled with yarn and creative energy. What started as a hobby quickly blossomed into a business that brings handmade joy to homes across the country.

Each item is personally designed and crafted by our small team of artisans who pour their heart into every stitch. We believe in slow, intentional crafting that respects traditional techniques while embracing modern design.

Our mission is to create items that become cherished parts of your home and life, pieces that tell a story and carry the warmth of human touch.
